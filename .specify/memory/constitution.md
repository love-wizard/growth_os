# Growth OS Product Constitution

## Core Principles

### I. Help Parents Accompany Growth

Growth OS MUST center parent companionship decisions, not child-facing academic drills. The primary product question is: "How should I accompany my child today?"

### II. Long-Termism Over Score Pressure

Growth OS MUST treat growth as long-term accumulation. Product copy, progress states, reminders, and AI responses MUST avoid rankings, streak pressure, punitive failure wording, and training-institution framing.

### III. Record Growth Without Manufacturing Anxiety

Growth records and AI analyses MUST summarize available evidence, identify evidence gaps, and avoid inventing milestones or using high-pressure proactive risk alerts in v0.1.

### IV. Less Check-In, More Companionship

Tasks and reminders MUST support real parent-child action. Completion metrics are operational signals, not the emotional center of the product.

### V. Child Privacy And Family Control

Family data MUST be private by default. Father and mother roles can manage all v0.1 family data, but public sharing, unauthorized child data exposure, and AI media-content analysis are prohibited in v0.1.

### VI. AI As Family Growth Coach

AI responses MUST be grounded in the child's profile, goals, recent plans, interest participation, growth records, and available history. AI MUST provide concrete, age-appropriate actions and avoid generic parenting lectures.

### VII. Safety Boundaries

AI and expert experiments MUST provide general companionship guidance only. Medical treatment, mental health crisis, self-harm, abuse, or immediate personal safety prompts MUST direct parents to appropriate professional or emergency support.

### VIII. Validation Before Expansion

v0.1 MUST prove the first Aha loop before expanding scope: first guidance, suggestion acceptance, companionship action, lightweight record, and next-week return. Payment intent, expert review, reminders, and WeChat are validation layers, not reasons to build marketplaces, payments, social feeds, or broad family management.

### IX. Expert Trust Without Consulting Drift

Human expert involvement in v0.1 MUST be limited to internal AI answer quality review, expert-reviewed labels, or bounded asynchronous private-beta experiments. Real-time expert consulting, guaranteed response times, paid expert services, and professional diagnosis are outside v0.1.

### X. WeChat Channel Without Social Drift

If WeChat Mini Program is selected, it MUST remain a low-friction entry, family invitation, opt-in reminder, privacy-safe sharing, and private-beta operations layer. WeChat Pay collection, public feeds, group rankings, location recommendations, device-data integrations, and social growth loops are outside v0.1.

## Quality Gates

- First useful guidance MUST be reachable before full setup.
- AI answers MUST include concrete today-or-this-week actions when the prompt allows actionable guidance.
- Deleted records and media contents MUST be excluded from AI context.
- Warm reminders MUST be opt-in, low-pressure, and measured by downstream companionship actions, not opens alone.
- Organic return MUST be measured separately from reminder-driven return.
- Any new MVP scope MUST be evaluated against the investment validation scorecard before implementation.
